Welcome to RaveBase

We’re a group of students working together on a DBMS based Event Management System as part of our learning journey. Our goal is to understand how real-world applications handle data from event registrations to attendee management by designing a fully functional database driven system. This project helps us apply what we've learned in class to a practical scenario, while building skills in teamwork, database design, and problem-solving.
